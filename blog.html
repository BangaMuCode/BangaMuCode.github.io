STUDENT INVOLVEMENT
Having a school activities at the University is one of the memorable experience that the students may encounter in their college life, having a school activities may reduce the stressors of students and given them the time to relax. Here is my favorite school activites in the University.
Welcome Ceremony of BUKSU
The welcome ceremony of buksu for the 1st years students is one of the memorable moment especially to 1st year students, its a one week celebration and the students are enjoyiing the different kinds of activities, each organization made by the students have their own unique of style and we as students we feel of relief and excitement for the school.
PE Week
PE week at BUKSU is one of my list of favorite activities done by the PE Orgranization. In this activites all students are involved in participating the said event and each section have their own style of performance. Our performance serves as our final exam on Our PathFit Subject.
Intramurals
School is not complete without Intramurals this activity let the students show off their skills in any various kinds of sports, and even talents like dancing, Singing, photgraphy and many more. this not only able to compete but able to make friends from the other organization, and let the school alive.
